Steak

Description

Steak, just steak by itself, who doesnt love steak... vegetarians and vegans I guess but still plain old steak.

This particular dish is pretty boring but full of vital nutrients and vitamins to help you grow big and strong, maybe you like the gym and feel like choming down a big old steak will fuel your workout, maybe you've had a bad day and just want some steak to cheer yoou up???


Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Cow Meat
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Chop your perfect size steak
  2. Leave to warm up to room temprature
  3. Heat oil in a Pan
  4. Throw steak in pan
  5. Cook to your liking
  6. Optional: Add sauce
  7. Enjoy!
