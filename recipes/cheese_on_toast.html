Cheese on toast

Description

Cheese on toast is a favorite comfort food of many families around the globe, originally a snack it can be adapted to a main meal by the addition of such things as

  • Sausages
  • Bacon
  • Ham

The above is not an exhaustive list but you can see the idea.


Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Butter
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Toast Bread
  2. Butter Bread
  3. Cut cheese and place on toasty bread
  4. Grill until cheese starts too brown
  5. Enjoy
